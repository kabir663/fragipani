Name of Tree

  • Common Name: PLUMERIA RUBRA
  • Scientific Name: Red Frangipani

The red frangipani (Plumeria rubra) is a tropical flowering plant known for its vibrant red to pink flowers with a sweet fragrance. Native to Central America and the Caribbean, it thrives in warm climates and well-drained soil. Often grown as an ornamental tree, it can reach up to 6 meters in height and is popular in gardens and landscaping.
